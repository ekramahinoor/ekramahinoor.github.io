Mahinoor Ekram

I am an MPhil student in Peace and Conflict Studies at UiT – The Arctic University of Norway. I hold a BSc in Environmental Science and Management from North South University, graduating Summa Cum Laude with a CGPA of 3.83/4.00.

My research lies at the intersection of humanitarian action, environmental policy, and climate justice, with interests in sustainable resource management, conflict resolution, and equitable solutions for vulnerable communities.
